A Briefly Unauthorized Message

If this page arrived without a click,
No need for panic—just a small trick.
A friendly hello, sent with a grin,
By someone who knows where you’ve been.

No secrets here, no stories to tell,
Just wishing you’re doing remarkably well.
Life moves fast, and paths may bend,
But some connections simply… extend.

So consider this note a well-timed cheer,
From a voice you might still recognize here.
No need to reply, no action required—
Just proof that good friendships never expire.
